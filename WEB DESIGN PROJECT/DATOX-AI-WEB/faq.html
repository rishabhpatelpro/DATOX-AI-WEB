    • Frequently asked questions

  • What is an FAQ section?
    1. An FAQ section can be used to quickly answer common questions about your business like "Where do you ship to?", "What are your opening hours?", or
      "How can I book a service?".
  • Why do FAQs matter?
    1. FAQs are a great way to help site visitors find quick answers to common questions about your business and create a better navigation experience.
  • Where can I add my FAQs?
    1. FAQs can be added to any page on your site or to your Wix mobile app, giving access to members on the go.
